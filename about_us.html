Sustainable Resources

Elegance Jewelry is committed to using only ethically and sustainably sourced gold and diamonds.

We collaborate with trusted suppliers who implement responsible mining practices. The diamonds we use come from sources certified by Kimberley Process, A global initiative that ensures diamonds do not come from conflict zones.

The gold we use comes from mines that adhere to the principles of Fairmined dan Fairtrade, ensuring the well-being of miners while protecting the environment.
